The World of Radfall

Radfall takes place after a catastrophic nuclear power plant explosion in 2029. Scientists working on Project Helix attempted to create a cure that would slow aging and extend human life. But the radiation from the reactor mutated the cure into a virus that twisted humans into Echoforms.

Building Radfall in Unreal Engine

Built entirely using Blueprints, Radfall features modular systems for movement, quests, survival, radiation mechanics, and AI. No C++ was used ‚Äî every mechanic is visually engineered for flexibility and iteration.
