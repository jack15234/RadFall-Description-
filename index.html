The World of Radfall

Radfall takes place after a catastrophic nuclear power plant explosion in 2029. Scientists working on Project Helix attempted to create a cure that would slow aging and extend human life. But the radiation from the reactor mutated the cure into a virus that twisted humans into Echoforms.

Building Radfall in Unreal Engine

Built entirely using Blueprints, Radfall features modular systems for movement, quests, survival, radiation mechanics, and AI. No C++ was used ‚Äî every mechanic is visually engineered for flexibility and iteration.

The Core Story

Players take on the role of Alex Rainer, searching for his sister, Dr. Elara Rainer, who vanished after the Helix virus mutated following the nuclear plant explosion.

Alex begins in the Fringe, learning basic survival, scavenging, crafting, and combat against early-stage Echoforms like Lurkers, Wailers, and Shrieker. The environment is dark, dangerous, and full of abandoned structures hiding clues to the virus‚Äôs origin.

Deadlight Fields ‚Äì The Heart of Mutation

In the Deadlight Fields, radiation spikes are extreme. Striders and Bleachers roam open zones, while Sirens and The Choir manipulate sound to confuse and disorient. Alex must navigate crumbling labs, irradiated forests, and collapsed towns to gather critical research data and uncover the failings of Project Helix.

The Core ‚Äì Confronting Kryne

The Core is the origin of the Helix mutation. Alex confronts Dr. Kryne, who has become a hybrid Echoform. Extreme radiation, unstable terrain, and deadly traps challenge the player. Choices here determine multiple endings, balancing morality, survival, and uncovering the truth.

Note: Echoforms retain fragments of their past, forcing Alex to choose between mercy and survival.

The Emotional Journey

Journals, recordings, and environmental storytelling immerse players in a tragic yet compelling world, emphasizing consequences of unchecked ambition, human resilience, and the haunting echoes of the failed cure.
